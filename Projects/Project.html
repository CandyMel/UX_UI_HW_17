Sprout: Budgeting App

A financial budgeting app to help users control their spending at any time by using a positive reinforcement to motivate the user to be aware in order to become financially responsible.

The Process:

Sprout is a mobile app that is a convenient way to be financially aware. Financial budgeting app to help users who don’t normally budget to solve their overspending, anxiety over money, and lack of financial awareness. Sprout give users control over their spending at any time, using positive reinforcement to motivate the user to be responsible in order to become financially independent.

In this research project, we planned and conducted:
Six (6) interviews
Online users surveys (22 responses)
Four (4) iterations
Three (3) user testing on digital prototype


The Problem:

During interviews and surveying for research about money management among users we discovered that money-conscious individuals are struggling to manage their finances because their current way of tracking spending is arduous, relies on mostly physical mediums which are easily lost and requires mental calculations to understand their holistic financial picture.



The Solution

A financial budgeting app to help users who don’t normally budget solve their overspending, anxiety over money, and lack of financial awareness. We give users control over their spending at any time and we use positive reinforcement to motivate the user to be aware in order to become financially responsible.



Who Uses Sprout?

Users who want to achieve financial stability for important milestones such as:

  • Going back to school and earn her Master’s Degree
  • Buy a House
  • Saving up for Retirement
  • Have money for emergencies

    • User Needs and expects:

  • An at-a-glance view of her expenses and budget
  • A budgeting and expense tracking product that lives up to the ease of her banking app
  • Increase and improve her financial planning skills



    • How does Sprout help?

    App can help by creating a way for users to easily track and monitor their spending while providing plans and limitations so they can stick to a budget. Sprout intends to motivate users tSo save effectively while having the freedom to spend appropriately by setting monthly limits.
